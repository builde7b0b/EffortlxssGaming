About Effortlxss

Starting back in 2005, a small group of dedicated friends brought together by gaming decided to start a team on a pretty popular online game at the time which was Rainbow Six Vegas: 2 on The Playstation 3 Platform!
The main members from that sqadron whose clan tag was "GOD", because we knew that we were all blessed to be able to be gaming and having a good time with like minded people from across the world, decided to keep that legacy going under a different name and brand and using a different medium. .
